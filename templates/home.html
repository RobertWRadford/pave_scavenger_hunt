Instructions

To play, log in or create an account to save your progress and give us your contact information. Your information will only be used to contact you at the end of the semester to ensure you receive your prize. The hunt consists of riddles, puzzles, and a few other thinkers as you progress through campus and city. You can either play entirely online by having google maps open on the side, or you may connect via data in-person on your phone. Enter your answer and press the "submit" button at the bottom of the screen. If you're stuck, you can find a hint option to help you move along.

This scavenger hunt takes approximately 1-2 hours online and about 6-8 hours in-person. To fully enjoy an in-person experience, we recommend you break the hunt into several parts, and bring a friend or two. To exit, simply exit the site and your progress will automatically be saved. Your name and email will be entered into a raffle at the end of the semester, and we'll contact you if you win. We wish you a fantastic time! A "Scavenger Hunt" page link will be in the header once you sign in, and you can start your hunt from there!
